Physics and Medicine in Biology 2017 Rotblat prize

The main STIR paper

Kris Thielemans, Charalampos Tsoumpas, Sanida Mustafovic, Tobias Beisel, Pablo Aguiar, Nikolaos Dikaios, and Matthew W Jacobson,
STIR: Software for Tomographic Image Reconstruction Release 2,
Physics in Medicine and Biology, 57 (4), 2012 pp.867-883.
was awarded the 2017 Physics in Medicine and Biology Rotblat Prize. This prize is awarded yearly to the research paper that has received the most citations in the preceding five years (according to the Institute for Scientific Information (ISI)). Check the announcement and some background info.

We'd like to thank all the authors of the paper for their work, and the STIR community for using STIR, and of course for citing the paper!
