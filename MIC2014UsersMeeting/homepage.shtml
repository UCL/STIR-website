STIR User's Meeting at MIC 2014

We had a succesful User's Meeting at the 2014 IEEE MIC, attended by 35-40 people. Some of the presentations can be found below.

Chairs

  • Charalampos Tsoumpas, University of Leeds
  • Kris Thielemans, University College London and Algorithms and Software Consulting Ltd

Where and when

IEEE Medical Imaging Conference, Seattle, WA, USA
Thursday, 13 November, 18:30-20:30, room 303
