STIR User's Meeting at MIC 2013

We had a succesful User's Meeting at the 2013 IEEE MIC, attended by 30-35 people.

Chairs

  • Charalampos Tsoumpas, University of Leeds
  • Kris Thielemans, University College London and Algorithms and Software Consulting Ltd

Where and when

IEEE Medical Imaging Conference, Seoul, South Korea
Thursday the 31st of October, 19:00-21:00
